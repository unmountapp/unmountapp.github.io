Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information (device type, operating system, unique device identifiers)
  • Usage data (features used, session duration, crash reports)
  • Analytics data to improve app performance

We use the information we collect to:

UnMount may access or collect health-related data such as workout information, exercise logs, and fitness metrics. This data is used solely to provide app functionality.
